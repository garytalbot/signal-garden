Signal Garden

Click anywhere to plant a glowing bloom. Every signal grows its own shape, color, and ridiculous little name. Now you can replay the whole thing or hand someone a link to the exact garden you made.

How it works

Each bloom is assembled from layered rings, stems, sparks, and a tiny procedural title. It’s half garden, half sci-fi field recorder.

Prompt

Try planting dense clusters in one corner, then a single lonely bloom far away. The garden feels different when it gets crowded.

Field log

The garden now keeps a tiny running diary of your session and can preserve the whole layout in a shareable link.
